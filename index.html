Project Manager

Position Summary
The Project Manager is responsible for the end-to-end delivery of construction projects undertaken by the Company as a Main Contractor. The role demands strong technical knowledge combined with commercial awareness to plan, execute, monitor, and close projects on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards. The candidate will manage projects including residential buildings, warehouses, cold stores, infrastructure roads, landscape works, and complete underground utility networks.

🏭

Industrial warehouses and cold storage facilities

Hotels and serviced apartments

Road works and landscape development

Developments for government and private sector clients

Key Responsibilities
1

Site Leadership & Team Management

  • Lead and direct site teams including site engineers, foremen, HSE officers, QC inspectors, and subcontractors
  • Oversee all civil, structural, architectural, MEP, and infrastructure works throughout the construction lifecycle
  • Conduct regular site walks, progress meetings, and coordination sessions
2

Subcontractor & Supplier Management

  • Manage subcontractor and supplier performance, ensuring timely delivery of materials and works
  • Review and approve subcontractor interim payment certificates (IPCs) and vendor invoices
  • Monitor quality and productivity across all trade contractors on site
3

Budget & Cost Control

  • Manage project budgets, cash flow forecasting, and cost-to-complete reporting on a monthly basis
  • Minimize cost overruns through proactive risk identification and value engineering
  • Ensure timely submission of client progress invoices and follow-up on receivables
4

Technical Coordination

  • Coordinate with Quality Engineer, Project Engineers & Technical Office for shop drawing submissions
  • Manage material approval requests (MARs) and RFI (Request for Information) workflows
  • Ensure technical compliance across civil, structural, architectural, and MEP disciplines
5

Variations & Change Orders

  • Identify, evaluate, and manage variations/change orders in coordination with the Contracts team
  • Document scope changes and negotiate commercial impact with clients and consultants
  • Maintain full audit trail of all contractual variations
6

Client & Project Closure

  • Serve as the primary point of contact for clients and consultants
  • Coordinate final inspections, snagging, and defect rectification to achieve Practical Completion
  • Ensure smooth project handover with all documentation and as-built drawings
Candidate Profile

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering, Construction Management, or related discipline
  • Proven experience managing construction projects as a Main Contractor
  • Strong technical knowledge across civil, structural, architectural, MEP, and infrastructure works
  • Hands-on experience with residential, warehouse, cold store, road, and underground utility projects
  • Commercial awareness — budgets, cash flow, IPCs, and variations/change orders
  • Experience with shop drawings, MARs, and RFI management
  • Strong leadership skills to manage site engineers, foremen, QC/HSE teams, and subcontractors
  • Excellent communication in English & Arabic preferred

What You Will Gain

  • Full ownership of end-to-end project delivery as Main Contractor
  • Exposure to diverse project types — residential, industrial, hospitality, infrastructure
  • Work on mixed-use developments for government and private sector clients
  • Lead multi-disciplinary teams across civil, MEP, and infrastructure trades
  • Drive commercial outcomes through cost control, value engineering, and variation management
  • Competitive package commensurate with experience and market standards
